General Information

The 20th Meeting of the Society for Natural Immunity (SNI) will be held in Oslo, Norway.

The meeting will take place at the Scandic Holmenkollen Park Hotel.

ABSTRACT PRESENTATIONS

All accepted abstracts, regardless of presentation type, will be presented as Posters.

Oral Abstract Presentations

Oral abstract presentations will take place in Saga Hall. Each abstract presenter will have 10 minutes total: 8 minutes for a formal presentation and 2 minutes for questions and answers. All presenters are asked to upload their slides with the AV technician in Saga Hall between 6:00 am – 8:00 am on the day of their presentation.

MEALS

All coffee breaks, meals, the Welcome Reception and the Gala dinner are included in the regular registration rate.The exception is the dinner at the Fram Museum on Wednesday, September 27th. For those not attending the Fram museum, dinner can be purchased at the hotel. Meal times and locations can be found within the detailed program schedule.

SOCIAL EVENTS

Welcome Reception
The Welcome Reception will take place on Tuesday, September 26th from 6:00 pm until 7:30 pm

Visit to Fram Museum – Ticket Required
The visit to the Fram museum will take place on Wednesday, September 27th from 5:30 pm until 8:00 pm.

Gala Dinner
The Gala Dinner will take place on Thursday, September 28th from 6:30 pm.

Drink tickets for social events will be included within registration packets. For additional drinks, a cash bar will be available at all events.

WEATHER

The average temperature in September is between 8°C (45°F) and 15°C (60°F). It rains often. It may occasionally snow. Wear layers!
